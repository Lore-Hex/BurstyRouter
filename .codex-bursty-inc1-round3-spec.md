## Blocker
1. **TR upstream must not inherit SDK timeout/retry magic** (a proxied SSE
   stream is hard-killed at 120s; retries would re-send non-idempotent
   generations and double-bill). In `NewTrustedRouterWithHTTPClient`: set
   `Options{MaxRetries: ptr(0), Timeout: ptr(time.Duration(0))}` and in
   `TrustedRouter.Chat` set `CallOptions.Timeout = ptr(time.Duration(0))`.
   Lifecycle is governed solely by the inbound request context. Comment WHY
   (proxy semantics: the client owns retries; streams must live as long as
   the client waits). Test: fake TR that streams a chunk, sleeps 200ms,
   streams another — with an SDK-default client this died at the per-attempt
   deadline; assert full passthrough (use a tiny Timeout on a CONTROL client
   to prove the mechanism, then assert the production constructor passes 0).
   Also assert the fake TR sees the POST exactly ONCE when it 500s
   (no retry).
